mission statement

GAC community members come from different countries, speak multiple languages, and work different jobs... but we share a passion: gaming and NFTs. This is a common denominator that unites us regardless of our backgrounds or circumstances.

GAC's mission is to strengthen the bridge between traditional gaming and NFTs by providing tangible value and benefits for holders, as well as integration with metaverse and P2E communities.

GAC is and will always be community first. Priority number one is to provide value back to holders and to provide a positive experience for the general community.
