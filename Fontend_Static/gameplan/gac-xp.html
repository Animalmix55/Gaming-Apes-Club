Game Plan V1

GAC XP


HOW TO EARN:

GAC community members and holders can gain GAC XP; an off-chain token. Holders will have significantly expanded GAC XP opportunities through diamond handing, gaming-to-earn tournaments, challenges, community involvement, and Discord.

Holders will accrue GAC XP at a higher multiple than non-holders and will be granted exclusive access to the GAC Shack.

GAC SHACK:

10% of mint will be dedicated to providing holders with gaming-to-earn opportunities, internal and external whitelist opportunities, products, and giveaways.
