Game Plan V1

GAC Launchpad

Facilitating the development of Web3 innovation is a crucial component of the GAC game plan. The future is now and we envision the GAC community leading the charge.

EMERGING PROJECTS:

Providing capital to community members that embody GAC principles. GAC will assist in the development of emerging NFT projects that align with our passion for gaming and NFTs.

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS:

Launch pad-developed partnerships and collaborations will provide priority benefits for GAC holders and will further immerse the GAC community into the Web3 world.
